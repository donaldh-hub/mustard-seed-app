MUSTARD SEED — WEEKLY FOLLOW-UP SYSTEM (FINALIZED)

Add a structured Weekly Follow-Up feature that triggers automatically every 7 days from user onboarding or from the last completed weekly review timestamp.

This feature is mandatory.
It is written.
It is structured.
It is not conversational chat.
It does not assign steps.
It does not redesign strategy.
It does not include water logic.
It does not include gamification.

The Weekly Follow-Up contains exactly three sections:

1) Targeted Goal Progress
2) Heartbeat Direction
3) Collective Analysis (maximum 4 sentences)

--------------------------------------------------
TRIGGER + HOME SCREEN BANNER
--------------------------------------------------

• Store weekly_cycle_start_timestamp at onboarding.
• Trigger Weekly Follow-Up every 7 days.
• When triggered, display a Home Screen banner:

  "Your Weekly Review is Ready"

• Banner remains visible until review is acknowledged.
• User taps banner to open full Weekly Review screen.
• Review must be acknowledged before banner disappears.
• Do not reset weekly cycle until review is completed.

--------------------------------------------------
CALENDAR INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------

• On the day the Weekly Review is generated, automatically add an entry to the in-app calendar:

  Title: Weekly Review
  Type: System Event
  Status: Completed (after acknowledgment)

• This calendar entry is permanent and viewable in history.
• Each review should be timestamped and archived.

--------------------------------------------------
WEEKLY REVIEW SCREEN (WRITTEN REPORT)
--------------------------------------------------

The Weekly Review must display as a structured written report.
Not chat bubbles.
Not conversational formatting.

Clean layout:

--------------------------------
WEEKLY REVIEW
--------------------------------

SECTION 1 — TARGETED GOAL PROGRESS

If a targeted goal exists, display:

• Goal statement
• Last week measurable value
• Current measurable value
• Net change

Example:

Goal: Lose 20 lbs by June 1
Last Week: 197 lbs
This Week: 195.8 lbs
Net Change: -1.2 lbs

No commentary.
No emotional framing.

If no targeted goal exists:

Display:
"No targeted goal active."

--------------------------------
SECTION 2 — HEARTBEAT DIRECTION
--------------------------------

Evaluate the previous 7 days of behavioral activity only.

Emotional language alone does not qualify as advancement.
Declared intention without execution does not qualify as advancement.

For each of the Five Heartbeats:

• Clarity of Vision & Why
• Small Steps + Consistency
• Mindset over Method
• Feedback & Adaptation
• Courageous Action

Assign exactly one direction:

↑ Advancement
→ Stagnation
↓ Regression

Display format:

Clarity of Vision & Why — ↑
Small Steps + Consistency — →
Mindset over Method — ↑
Feedback & Adaptation — →
Courageous Action — ↓

No explanation under arrows.

--------------------------------
HEARTBEAT EVALUATION CRITERIA
--------------------------------

Clarity of Vision & Why

↑ Advancement:
• Goal defined or refined
• Purpose recommitted
• Objective clarified

→ Stagnation:
• No refinement, no confusion introduced

↓ Regression:
• Goal abandoned
• Contradictory direction
• Loss of clarity

--------------------------------

Small Steps + Consistency

↑ Advancement:
• Repeated execution across multiple days
• Follow-through on commitments

→ Stagnation:
• Minimal or inconsistent action without full drop-off

↓ Regression:
• Repeated missed commitments
• Execution collapse

--------------------------------

Mindset over Method

↑ Advancement:
• Action taken despite discomfort
• Reduced excuse behavior
• Continued execution without perfect conditions

→ Stagnation:
• Neutral behavioral pattern

↓ Regression:
• Excuse patterns dominate
• Avoidance disguised as logic

--------------------------------

Feedback & Adaptation

↑ Advancement:
• Strategy adjusted after friction
• Intelligent modification applied

→ Stagnation:
• No adaptation required or applied

↓ Regression:
• Repeated friction with no adjustment

--------------------------------

Courageous Action

↑ Advancement:
• Action taken on previously avoided task
• Initiated difficult step

→ Stagnation:
• No avoidance addressed

↓ Regression:
• Backed away from declared action
• Avoided necessary step

--------------------------------
SECTION 3 — COLLECTIVE ANALYSIS
--------------------------------

Generate one short integrated written analysis.

Rules:

• Maximum 4 sentences
• No step assignment
• No plan creation
• No motivational padding
• No water logic
• No gamification language

The analysis must synthesize:

• Targeted Goal Progress
• Overall heartbeat pattern
• Alignment or misalignment between outcome and development

Tone:

Clear
Direct
Grounded
Objective
Non-therapeutic

--------------------------------
POST-COMPLETION BEHAVIOR
--------------------------------

• Upon acknowledgment, archive the review.
• Add it to Review History.
• Display a compact summary snapshot on Home Screen:

  Last Weekly Review
  Goal Net: [value]
  Heartbeats: ↑ → ↑ → ↓

• Begin next 7-day cycle immediately.

End Weekly Follow-Up System.
