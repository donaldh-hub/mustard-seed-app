Mustard Seed is NOT a fitness app.

# 2. Permanent Product Rule

Wire this product rule into app copy, Jai/JI behavior, and internal guidance:

“Mustard Seed does not reward workouts. It rewards aligned action.”

Aligned action means the user took a real step toward the goal they created and the WHY they attached to it.

Examples:

1. If the goal is fitness, a gym photo may count.
2. If the goal is gardening, fertilizer in the cart may count.
3. If the goal is writing, a draft page may count.
4. If the goal is budgeting, a completed budget sheet may count.
5. If the goal is business, product photos or a website update may count.
6. If the goal is studying, notes or completed practice may count.
7. If the goal is cleaning, one cleared room or organized drawer may count.
8. If the goal is faith, a journal entry or study plan may count.

The question is not:

“Did this user work out?”

The question is:

“Did this user take a real step toward what they said mattered?”
