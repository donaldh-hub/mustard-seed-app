1. Whether water is earned

Not emotion.
Not intention.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — ACTIVE GOAL STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------

• Each goal has its own water container.
• Each goal has its own cups.
• Each goal has its own seed.
• Water does NOT transfer between goals.
• Cups do NOT transfer between goals.
• Growth is tied strictly to that specific goal.

If a goal is completed or abandoned:
→ Its seed stops growing.
→ A new goal starts a new seed.

Growth reflects discipline toward THAT goal only.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — HOW WATER IS EARNED
--------------------------------------------------

Water is awarded ONLY when the user takes measurable action aligned with their active goal.

If they TALK about it → No water.
If they DO it → Water.

Water is awarded for:
• Completing a stated step
• Executing something previously committed to
• Demonstrable progress toward the goal
• Improvement over previous baseline
• Courageous action tied to the goal
• Weekly recap confirming real movement

Water is NOT awarded for:
• General conversation
• Venting
• Thinking about action
• Planning without execution
• Emotional state alone
• Being “on the fence”

No fake logging.
No dopamine farming.
No intention rewards.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — WATER QUANTITY RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Base Action:
• 1 water event

Photo + clear contextual proof:
• 2 water events

Photo must clearly relate to the goal.
Context must confirm execution.

Weekly Recap:
• 1 water per heartbeat where verified movement occurred.
• No movement in a heartbeat = No water for that heartbeat.

Water is evaluated per heartbeat independently.
No aggregate scoring model.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — SEED GROWTH STAGES (UNDERGROUND EMPHASIS MODEL)
--------------------------------------------------

Growth must visually emphasize underground development.
Root formation must be clearly visible.
Above-ground visibility must come later.

STAGE 0 — Dormant Seed
• Seed intact
• Fully underground
• Soil undisturbed

--------------------------------------

STAGE 1 — Germination (Unlocked after Cup 1)
• Seed visibly cracks
• Clear fracture line
• Slight internal glow
• Soil subtly shifts
• No roots visible yet

--------------------------------------

STAGE 2 — Primary Root Formation (Unlocked after Cup 2)
• One visible white root extends downward
• Soil parts beneath seed
• Crack widens slightly

Root must clearly grow downward.

--------------------------------------

STAGE 3 — Root Expansion (Unlocked after Cup 3)
• Root thickens
• Secondary root branches appear
• Underground network becomes visible
• Soil shows outward pressure

Still no sprout above ground.

--------------------------------------

STAGE 4 — Soil Pressure Phase (Unlocked after Cup 4)
• Root network established
• Soil mounds slightly upward
• Upward tension animation visible
• Energy building beneath surface

--------------------------------------

STAGE 5 — Sprout Emergence (Unlocked after Cup 5)
• Small green sprout breaks through soil
• Soil visibly parts
• Root system remains visible beneath surface
• Seed shell dissolves

--------------------------------------

STAGE 6 — Early Plant Stability (Unlocked after Cup 8 total)
• Stem strengthens
• First small leaves form
• Root network deepens
• Soil stabilizes around base

Later stages may scale gradually with additional cups.

Early growth progresses one stage per cup.
Later growth requires additional cups to reflect maturity.
