MUSTARD SEED — WEEKLY FOLLOW-UP ENGINE

Add a structured Weekly Follow-Up feature that triggers automatically every 7 days from user onboarding or from the last completed weekly review timestamp.

This feature is an audit only.
It does not assign steps.
It does not redesign strategy.
It does not include water logic.
It does not include gamification.

The Weekly Follow-Up must contain exactly three sections:

1) Targeted Goal Progress
2) Heartbeat Direction
3) Collective Analysis (maximum 4 sentences)

----------------------------------------
TRIGGER LOGIC
----------------------------------------

• Store weekly_cycle_start_timestamp at onboarding.
• Trigger Weekly Follow-Up every 7 days.
• If user does not complete review on Day 7, mark as “Pending Weekly Review.”
• Do not reset cycle until review is completed.
• After completion, reset weekly_cycle_start_timestamp.
• Store previous weekly measurable values for comparison.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — TARGETED GOAL PROGRESS
----------------------------------------

If a targeted goal exists, display:

• Goal statement
• Last week’s measurable value (from previous weekly review)
• Current measurable value
• Net change

Format example:

Goal: Lose 20 lbs by June 1
Last Week: 197 lbs
This Week: 195.8 lbs
Net Change: -1.2 lbs

No commentary.
No emotional interpretation.

If no targeted goal exists, display:

“No targeted goal active.”

----------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — HEARTBEAT DIRECTION
----------------------------------------

Evaluate the previous 7 days of user behavioral activity only.

Emotional language alone is not sufficient evidence.
Declared intention without execution is not advancement.

For each of the Five Heartbeats:

• Clarity of Vision & Why
• Small Steps + Consistency
• Mindset over Method
• Feedback & Adaptation
• Courageous Action

Assign exactly one direction:

↑ Advancement
→ Stagnation
↓ Regression

----------------------------------------
HEARTBEAT EVALUATION CRITERIA
----------------------------------------

1) Clarity of Vision & Why

↑ Advancement:
• Goal defined or refined
• Purpose restated or recommitted
• Measurable objective clarified

→ Stagnation:
• No refinement or confusion introduced

↓ Regression:
• Goal abandoned
• Contradictory direction
• Loss of clarity

----------------------------------------

2) Small Steps + Consistency

↑ Advancement:
• Repeated execution of declared steps
• Habit repetition across multiple days
• Follow-through on commitments

→ Stagnation:
• Minimal activity
• Inconsistent but not collapsed

↓ Regression:
• Repeated missed commitments
• Drop-off in execution
• Avoidance of declared actions

----------------------------------------

3) Mindset over Method

↑ Advancement:
• Action taken despite discomfort
• Continued execution without perfect conditions
• Reduction of excuse-based behavior

→ Stagnation:
• Neutral behavior
• No adversity faced or addressed

↓ Regression:
• Excuse patterns dominate
• Waiting for perfect timing
• Avoidance framed as logic

----------------------------------------

4) Feedback & Adaptation

↑ Advancement:
• Strategy adjusted after friction
• Intelligent modification of approach
• Active problem-solving

→ Stagnation:
• No adaptation needed or applied

↓ Regression:
• Repeated friction without adjustment
• Same mistake repeated
• Ignored performance feedback

----------------------------------------

5) Courageous Action

↑ Advancement:
• Action taken on something previously avoided
• Visible step taken despite hesitation
• Initiated difficult conversation or change

→ Stagnation:
• No avoidance pattern addressed

↓ Regression:
• Backed away from declared action
• Avoided known necessary step

----------------------------------------

Display format:

Clarity of Vision & Why — ↑
Small Steps + Consistency — →
Mindset over Method — ↑
Feedback & Adaptation — →
Courageous Action — ↓

No explanation under arrows.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — COLLECTIVE ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------

Generate one short integrated analysis.

Rules:

• Maximum 4 sentences
• No step assignment
• No new plan creation
• No micro-action recommendation
• No motivational padding
• No water logic
• No gamification language

The analysis must synthesize:

• Targeted Goal Progress
• Overall heartbeat pattern
• Alignment or misalignment between outcome and development

Tone:

Clear
Direct
Grounded
Objective
Non-therapeutic

End Weekly Follow-Up.
