Enhance the water reward system so that every time a user earns water, the action is visibly reflected in the water cup next to the seed AND acknowledged by Jae in the chat.

Current issue:
Water rewards may occur internally but the user does not always see or hear confirmation that water was added.

Required behavior:

Whenever the system awards water to the user (from any valid reward event), the following must happen in the same flow:

1. Add the correct amount of water to the user's cup container
2. Immediately update the cup UI next to the seed so the water level visibly rises
3. Trigger a short visual water-fill animation in the cup
4. Jae must send a short acknowledgement message in chat confirming the water reward

This must happen for all legitimate water events including:
- first goal planted
- verified action taken toward a goal
- proof-based progress (photos, evidence)
- milestone completions
- other valid rule-based rewards defined in the water matrix

Jae acknowledgement rules:

The message must be short and natural. Examples:

"Nice work. Water added to your cup."

or

"Good step forward. Water added to your cup."

Do not generate long explanations or multiple messages. The acknowledgement should be one short sentence confirming that water was added.

Important system rules:

- Every water reward must update the cup UI immediately
- Water must always appear in the cup next to the seed
- No water rewards should occur silently
- The chat acknowledgement must only trigger when water is actually added
- Casual conversation or non-progress chat must never trigger this message

Cup behavior requirements:

The cup must show clear fill levels:
0% empty
25% quarter full
50% half full
75% three-quarter full
100% full

When the cup reaches 100%, trigger the existing pour animation that waters the seed and resets the cup to empty.

Ensure the chat acknowledgement happens at the moment water is added to the cup, not after the seed is watered.

Do not simulate this behavior. Connect the acknowledgement directly to the real water reward logic.
